# Content Engine Blueprint

Diagnose which of the 5 content marketing phases you're in, set the right objective (brand building vs. direct response), and output a content plan with platform selection, posting frequency, content pillars, and phase-appropriate strategy.

Read `references/frameworks/content-as-lead-gen.md` for Hormozi's full content philosophy.

## Why This Matters

Most businesses treat content as an afterthought - posting randomly, measuring vanity metrics, and wondering why it doesn't generate customers. Hormozi identifies five distinct phases of building a content marketing machine, and doing phase-3 tactics in phase-1 is why most people fail.

> "And in this video, I want to break down the shift that I've had in terms of content marketing and its role within a business, the five phases in building a content marketing machine, and how to focus on one of two primary objectives with content marketing."
> — Alex Hormozi, "1.2M Followers in 6 Months"

The critical insight most people miss: content serves two completely different objectives, and confusing them kills your strategy.

> "The purpose of having the organic content is lead nurture, not lead generation. Phase one and phase two, if you're in one of those camps, you don't need to do anything else."
> — Alex Hormozi, "1.2M Followers in 6 Months"

## The Consultation

### Step 1: Assess Current State

Ask the user:

1. **What is your business and offer?**
2. **How many followers/subscribers do you have across all platforms?**
3. **How often do you currently post?** (Never, occasionally, 1-2x/week, daily, multiple daily)
4. **What platforms are you on?**
5. **Have you ever gotten a customer from content?** (If yes, how?)
6. **Do you have a team for content, or is it just you?**

### Step 2: Phase Diagnosis

Map them to Hormozi's 5 phases:

| Phase | What It Looks Like | Objective |
|-------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Phase 1: Post something** | You haven't started, or you post sporadically | Just start. Get reps. |
| **Phase 2: Post consistently** | You post 1-3x/week on one platform | Build the habit. Content = lead nurture only. |
| **Phase 3: Post on all platforms** | You post on a regular cadence across 2+ platforms | Test hooks, learn what resonates |
| **Phase 4: Invest in production** | You have systems, maybe a team, repurposing engine | Content becomes a real lead generation channel |
| **Phase 5: Full content machine** | Full team, daily content, multi-platform, data-driven | Content is your primary growth engine |

> "Phase one is you make something and you post it. And believe it or not, a lot of you guys haven't even done phase one, which is why I have to outline it."
> — Alex Hormozi, "1.2M Followers in 6 Months"

Tell the user their phase. Be honest. Most people are in phase 1 or 2 and think they should be doing phase 4 tactics.

### Step 3: Set the Objective

There are exactly two objectives for content, and you must pick one per phase:

| Objective | What It Means | When to Use |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Lead Nurture** | Content warms up people who already know you | Phases 1-2 (always), alongside outreach |
| **Lead Generation** | Content attracts new people who discover you | Phases 3-5 (only when you can invest real time) |

> "If you're doing phase 3, 4, 5, the objective is to generate new customers from content, and it's different than phase 1 and phase 2, where the only reason you can do that is just to have lead nurture."
> — Alex Hormozi, "1.2M Followers in 6 Months"

If someone is in phase 1-2 and spending 4 hours/day on content instead of outreach, they have the wrong priority. Content supports outreach; it does not replace it until phase 3+.

### Step 4: Choose Platforms

| Platform | Best For | Content Type | Posting Frequency |
|----------|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| YouTube | Long-form authority, SEO, evergreen | Video (10-20 min) | 1-2x/week |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional services | Text posts, articles | 3-5x/week |
| Instagram | Visual brands, personal brands | Reels + carousel | 5-7x/week |
| Twitter/X | Thought leadership, networking | Threads + posts | 5-10x/week |
| TikTok | Attention, younger demo | Short video | 1-3x/day |
| Podcast | Deep engagement, trust building | Audio (30-60 min) | 1x/week |

Hormozi's principle: pick ONE platform to master first. Only expand after you have a consistent rhythm.

> "If you want to make content marketing your actual lead generation machine, then you need to invest in it the same way you invest in it for paid marketing."
> — Alex Hormozi, "1.2M Followers in 6 Months"

### Step 5: Define Content Pillars

Every business needs 3-5 content pillars that map to what their ideal customer cares about. The pillars are NOT about your product. They are about the problems your customer has.

| Pillar Type | Example (Fitness Business) | Example (B2B Agency) |
|-------------|---------------------------|---------------------|
| **Problem education** | "Why diets fail" | "Why most ads don't convert" |
| **Process reveal** | "How our clients lose 20 lbs" | "How we run ad creative tests" |
| **Proof/results** | "Client transformation stories" | "Case study: 3x ROAS in 60 days" |
| **Personal/relatable** | "What I eat in a day" | "Mistakes I made scaling my agency" |
| **Perspective/opinion** | "Unpopular take on keto" | "Why I hate vanity metrics" |

Hormozi's content philosophy:

> "Give in public, sell in private."
> — Alex Hormozi, "I Helped Business Owners Overcome Their Fear of Failure"

All public content is value. All selling happens in DMs, calls, and private conversations.

### Step 6: Build the Posting Cadence

Based on their phase, create a realistic posting schedule:

**Phase 1-2 (Nurture only):**
- 1-2 posts/week on primary platform
- Spend 80% of time on outreach, 20% on content
- Content recaps wins, shares insights, shows work

**Phase 3 (Testing):**
- 3-5 posts/week on primary + repurpose to secondary
- Start tracking what resonates (saves, shares, DMs)
- Test different formats (video, text, carousel)

**Phase 4-5 (Lead Gen Machine):**
- Daily posts on primary, 3-5x on secondary platforms
- Repurposing system (1 long-form → 5-10 short-form pieces)
- Data-driven: track leads generated per content piece
